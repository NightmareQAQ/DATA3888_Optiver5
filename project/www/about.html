About

Throughout the course of this project, the overall goal was to develop a tool to aid traders in interpreting and approaching the stock market via predicting the volatility of stocks using financial data. Volatility is considered as one of the most important factors in the financial world of trading as it highlights the potential for traders to turn a profit; to predict it would be not only fundamental for first time users in approaching the stock market but also for experienced traders in analysing past trends and perceive future trends of the market.
